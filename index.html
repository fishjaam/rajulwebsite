Meet Raj

Hi I'm Raj! I've played pickleball for 5 years and currently compete in professional pickleball (APP and PPA) tournaments across the country in the qualifying brackets. Last year I participated in events in Austin, Atlanta, Chicago, Vancouver Canada, and Arizona to name a few. My current DUPR rating is 5.55.

My background is in tennis and I have had a racquet in my hand since I was 5 and played competitively since I was 7 years old. I've played junior tournaments across the country and I was the #1 highschool recruit in Oregon for my grade. I've played all 4 years at the collegiate level.

I love teaching and working with people, and I take pride in creating a personalized experience for each client. My coaching style is adaptive and tailored to fit individual needs, because I believe no two players learn the same way.
